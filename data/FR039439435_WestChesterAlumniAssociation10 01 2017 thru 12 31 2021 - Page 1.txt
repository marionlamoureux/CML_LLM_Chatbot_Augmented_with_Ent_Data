THIS AFFINITY CREDIT CARD AGREEMENT (the ?Agreement?), is entered into the 1* day of
October, 2017, (the "Effective Date") by and between West Chester University Alumni Association,
with a mailing and physical address of, West Chester University Alumni Association, 202 Carter Drive,
West Chester, PA 19382, (hereafter referred to as ?WCUAA"), and Pennsylvania State Employees Credit
Union, with a mailing address of, PO Box 67013, Harrisburg, PA 17106-7013 and physically located at
1500 Elmerton Avenue, Harrisburg, PA 17110-2900 (?PSECU?) (together, the ?Parties?, or individually, a
?Party?).

WHEREAS, PSECU desires to be the exclusive provider of Affinity Credit Cards to WCUAA members; and

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to work together to promote an Affinity Credit Card to WCUAA members
(the "Program?).

NOW THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the foregoing and the mutual promises and intending to
be legally bound hereby, the Parties agree as follows.

1, RESPONSIBILITIES OF PSECU, PSECU, subject to the terms and conditions
set forth below, shat perform the following specified duties:

a. PSECU agrees to offer the following PSECU credit cards with WCUAA-approved branding to
WCUAA members:
1. Classic Visa? at the same rate of interest and fee structure as the Classic Visa offered
to PSECU?s general membership; and,
2. Founder?s Card at the same rate of interest and fee structure as the Founder?s Card
offered to PSECU?s general membership.

The terms and conditions, rates and promotional offers related to each of the above-listed
cards are subject to change at PSECU?s sole discretion.

b. For purposes of this Agreement, the WCUAA-branded Classic Visa and the WCUAA branded
Founder?s Card, together, with be referred to as the ?WCUAA Card(s)?. PSECU agrees to pay
WCUAA $50 for each new WCUAA Card opened by a WCUAA member who becomes a PSECU
member during the term of this Agreement. Renewals of existing credit cards obtained under
this Agreement will not be compensated.

c. PSECU will offer existing WCUAA members who are currently PSECU members who also have
PSECU credit cards (anyone who was a member of both WCUAA and PSECU prior to October 1,
2017) the opportunity to switch to the WCUAA Card. PSECU will pay WCUAA $10 for each
WCUAA member who switches to the WCUAA branded Card during the term of this Agreement.

d. PSECU will offer qualified West Chester University ("WCU") students a WCUAA Card once they
have graduated from WCU. WCUAA will provide a list of all graduating WCU students to PSECU
for solicitation of PSECU membership and products and services. PSECU will compensate
WCUAA $50 for each new WCUAA Card that is opened through the Program. For any WCU
student who is a PSECU member and is also a PSECU credit cardholder (both the PSECU
membership and PSECU cardholder status must have been established prior to October 1,
2017), when such student ?switches? to the WCUAA Card PSECU will compensate WCUAA $10
for any such switch which transpires during the term of Agreement.

e. PSECU shall deliver to WCUAA a quarterly report indicating the total number of WCUAA Cards
for that quarter, subject to this Agreement. PSECU will compensate WCUAA for each of the
above instances as stated in 1.b and 1.c above.

f. Payments shall be made on a quarterly basis via ACH transfer per instructions to be provided
by WCUAA to PSECU. In the alternative, payments may be made by check to WCUAA if WCUAA
notifies PSECU in writing of such request prior to an ACH payment being made.
