Attachment A

Elizabethtown College Financial Educational Center of Credit Union

Location. Dates and Hours of Operation.

The College and the Credit Union agree to retain the Financial Educational Center on
campus. The center will be located in the Brossman Commons Student Center
building at 1 Alpha Drive, Elizabethtown College, Elizabethtown, Pa. 17022. Dates
and hours of operation will be determined on an as needed basis.

Purpose of Financial Educational Center

Teach Elizabethtown College students, faculty and staff about Credit Union services;
promote Credit Union membership by one-on-one interactions at the Financial
Educational Center and telephone contacts; demonstrate how to use automated
delivery systems located in the Financial Educational Center; offer Students, Faculty
and Staff a comprehensive financial literacy program that can be used to promote
responsible monetary decision making throughout their lives. Assist those on campus
in the application process for Credit Union membership or services.

Expenses

Utilities - all but electric and janitorial services for the Financial Education Center
will be paid by Credit Union; electric and janitorial service expenses paid by
Elizabethtown College

Insurance - Credit Union shall maintain insurance coverage typical of rented space
and employee bonding for the Financial Education Center.

Equipment, supplies, brochures, applications and literature provided and paid by
Credit Union.

Membership application expense - $5 share purchase will be made on behalf of the

member applicant. If the member withdraws membership within the first year, $5
share will be retained by Credit Union.
