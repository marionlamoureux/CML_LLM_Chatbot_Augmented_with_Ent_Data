DESCRIPTION OF CU REWARDS PROGRAM

Christian Community Credit Union?s CURewards program is a rewards program where CCCU credit card
holders earn CU Points for eligible credit card purchases that are redeemable for a variety of redemptions
including airfare, hotels, merchandise, gift cards, and cash back. In addition, CCCU/Wildcat Rewards co-
branded card holders can redeem CU Points at a 1:1 ratio for Wildcat Rewards Points.

More information can be found at https://www.mycccu.com/curewards/ which includes a link to the program?s
terms and conditions.
