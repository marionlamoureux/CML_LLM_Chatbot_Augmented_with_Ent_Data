Addendum to the Harrisburg Area Community College, York Campus Financial
Educational Center Agreement dated October 1, 2014
Between Pennsylvania State Employees Credit Union & Harrisburg Area Community
College, York Campus

THIS ADDENDUM (the ?Addendum?), made this 8th day of July, 2020 (?Addendum Effective
Date?), between the Pennsylvania State Employees Credit Union (?PSECU? or ?Credit Union?),
and Harrisburg Area Community College, York Campus (?HACC?).

It is the desire of PSECU and HACC who are the same parties to the ?Harrisburg Area
Community College, York Campus Financial Educational Center Agreement?, along with any
addenda and exhibits, all of which were dated October 1, 2014, and were duly executed by
representatives of the aforementioned parties (referred to herein as the ?Agreement?), to extend
the term of the Agreement. NOW THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. The parties agree to extend the term of the Agreement for one year until August 31,
2021.

2. For the term of the Addendum, the parties agree Attachment B-Part 2 of the Agreement
is hereby addended to read as follows:

During the term of the Addendum, the parties agree to the following payments:

e Signing Bonus in the amount of $1,000.00 to be paid on or within thirty (30) days
after July 1, 2020.

e Scholarships to be paid to HACC Foundation in the amount of $3,000.00 to be paid
on or within thirty (30) days after July 1, 2020.

e Sponsorships to be paid to the HACC Foundation in the amount of $1,000.00 to be
paid on or within thirty (30) days after July 1, 2020.

e Orientation Support in the amount of $500.00 to be paid on or within thirty (30) days
after July 1, 2020.

e Rental Payment for Campus Space in the amount of $250.00 per month to begin
January 1, 2021. Rental Payments shall be paid on no later than thirty (30) days
after the end of each month for the preceding month. Notwithstanding the foregoing,
Rental Payments shall not be due for any month in which the physical campus and
Campus Space are not fully open to students.

The Agreement remains in full force and effect in accordance with its terms, except as herein
identified.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have caused this Addendum to the Agreement to
be executed and sealed by their duly authorized representatives as of the date first written
above.
