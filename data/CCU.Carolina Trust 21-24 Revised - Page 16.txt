Acceptable Advertising Verbiage

Carolina Trust Federal Credit Union will be permitted to use the following
verbiage in promoting their Coastal Carolina Affinity Card...

Examples ?
1) Get an official CCU Athletics Credit Card*
*Must contain ?An official partner of Coastal Carolina Athletics?, in ad copy.

2) Carolina Trust Federal Credit Union offers official CCU (Coastal Carolina) Athletics Credit Cards.*
*Must contain ?An official partner of Coastal Carolina Athletics?, in ad copy.

3) Carolina Trust is an official partner of CCU Athletics. Get your CCU (Coastal Carolina) Athletics credit
card, etc.

Plus, other verbiage may be used that is mutually agreed upon and University approved.
