CRITERIA FOR SELECTION

EVALUATION

All proposals will be reviewed and evaluated by a committee of University personnel. This
committee will recommend the proposal which most closely meets the requirements of
the RFP. Costs will only be evaluated after the other requirements have been met.
SELECTION CRITERIA

Proposals will be evaluated using the following criteria:

a. Completeness and clarity of the proposal and ability to meet the University's
needs

b. Contractor experience

c. References

d. Cost

e. Ability to meet timeline established in section 3

f. Value added services
