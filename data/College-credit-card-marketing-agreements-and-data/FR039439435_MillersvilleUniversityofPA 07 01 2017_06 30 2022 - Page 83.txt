Total Cost to Millersville University

PSECU shall continue to operate the Financial Education Center at no additional cost to Millersville
University. PSECU will be responsible for the installation and on-going maintenance associated with
conduit, electrical, computer, telephone and other data and phone lines which serve the Financial
Education Center and the ATMs, as well as any and all other facilities, equipment or furniture which
may be necessary or appropriate for the operation and maintenance of the Center. If PSECU is awarded
the contract, our planned renovation of the Center space will be at no cost to the University.

While there are no direct program costs to the University, there are expenses related to the program
that will be incurred by the University in support of program delivery. They are costs related to
providing:

? PSECU with interior wall and floor space in the mutually agreed upon location for use by the
Credit Union as the Financial Education Center

? Utilities for the operation of PSECU?s Financial Education Center and the on-campus ATMs

e Alternating current (A/C) power to approved ATM locations

are prepared and provided by PSECU at its sole cost and expense.
