During the term of this Agreement, Credit Union will pay Alumni Association a Royalty
pursuant to Section 5 of the Agreement calculated as follows, for those accounts with active
charging privileges.

A. Royalty Guarantee:

The Credit Union shall pay Alumni Association the following Royalty Guarantees for the first
ten (10) years following the Effective Date of this Agreement:

Year 1 Five Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars ($540,000)
Year 2 Five Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars ($540,000)
Year 3 Five Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars ($540,000)
Year 4 Five Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars ($540,000)
Year 5 Five Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars ($540,000)
Year 6 Five Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars ($540,000)
Year 7 Five Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars ($540,000)
Year 8 Five Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars ($540,000)
Year 9 Five Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars ($540,000)
Year 10 Five Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars ($540,000)

Each annual Royalty Guarantee outlined above shall be paid in four (4) equal installments, with
installments due on or before the forty-fifth day following the end of each calendar quarter.

B. GIP Account Bonus:

For each contract year during the term of this Agreement, Alumni Association will receive an
account bonus (Qualifying GIP Account Bonus) for any new GIP Accounts generated during that
contract year exceeding 500 based on the following chart. Qualifying GIP Account Bonus will
be calculated at the end of the first quarter of each contract year (starting in Year 2) for the
previous contract year, and if due, will be paid by Credit Union in a lump sum within 30 days.

Number of Annual GIP Accounts Exceeding 500

0-499 $0
500-999 $50,000
1000-1499 $100,000
1500-1999 $150,000
2000-2999 $200,000
3,000+ $300,000*

*UICCU will pay $50,000 for every 500 GIP Accounts exceeding 3,000 GIP Accounts in the
calendar year.
