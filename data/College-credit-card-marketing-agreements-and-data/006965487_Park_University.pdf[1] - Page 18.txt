CARD CUSTOMIZATION SERVICES

SERVICE DESCRIPTION
Background

1.1.1. The Card Customization Services will be a hosted (ASP) solution to enable Users using a
design interface to customize the appearance of their Card under the relevant Brand
Features.

1.1.2 You shall agree with Bank on a Configuration for incorporation into the Card Designer.
Following such agreement, Bank shall incorporate the Configuration into the Card Designer
and shall make the Card Designer available to the general public over the internet, and shall
host and support it during the Term.

1.1.3 You acknowledge and agree that the Minimum Specification is required in order to access
and use the Card Designer. Bank shall have no responsibility for ensuring that the
equipment of any User seeking to access or use the Card Designer complies with the
Minimum Specification.

Design Interface
1.2.1. The Card Designer will:

1.2.1.1. make available up to five categories of up to nine images each, which may be
used for Card production if the User does not opt to upload his or her own image;

1.2.1.2 allow the User to upload their own image(s);

1.2.1.3 allow the User to manipulate the image, (which may be either a stock gallery or
User uploaded image), using standard controls; and

1.2.1.4 generate a unique identifier assigned to each submitted card design, and include it
as a parameter into a return URL agreed between the Parties.

1.2.2 For the avoidance of doubt, you shall not be entitled to request any changes to the Card
Designer after the Configuration has been agreed between you and Bank.

Image Checking

1.3.1 Bank verifies that each image submitted by a User for incorporation into a Card for
compliance with the provisions of Section 4.7 of the Affinity Group Agreement, and retains
the right to reject any image that does not, in the Bank's view, meet the qualifications set
forth in that section of the Agreement. Bank?s determination in that regard is final.

CARDHOLDER FOR ANY LOSS SUFFERED BY YOU OR BY A CARDHOLDER IN
CONNECTION WITH ANY ACT OR OMISSION ARISING OUT OF ITS PERFORMANCE
(OR THAT OF ITS SUBCONTRACTORS) OF IMAGE CHECKING IN RELATION TO USER
PROVIDED IMAGES.
