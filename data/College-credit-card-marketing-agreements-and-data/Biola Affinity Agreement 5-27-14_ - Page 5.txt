Schedule of Royalties

Royalties for Visa Card Programs.

1. For each new Affinity Credit Card account opened, CCCU will pay $100 to Sponsor.
2. CCCU will pay to Sponsor a royalty based on the percentage of the net purchase volume
each calendar quarter according to the following schedule:

Number of Open Accounts Percentage of Net Purchase Volume
0-2,499 20%
2,500 22%
3,000 24%
3,500 26%
4,000 28%
4,500+ 30%
