Incentive Schedule

Incentives are payable for any of the services (listed in the chart below) established
at the Financial Educational Center upon opening. Incentives will be paid for existing
members adding the following services at the Financial Educational Center within
one (1) year of account opening.

Incentives are payable for membership or services requested as a result of
orientation events; special mailings by Elizabethtown College (postage paid and
materials provided by Credit Union); or if requested at the Financial Educational
Center. Incentives will be paid for existing members adding the following services at
the Financial Educational Center within one (1) year of account opening.

Elizabethtown College incentive payments will be made no later than the 25" of the
following month.

Financial Educational Center Management Intern and Financial Educational Center
Student Intern incentives are payable to the intern during his term of employment at
the Financial Educational Center. Student Intern incentive payments will be made as
a part of the normal Credit Union payroll process. No incentive will be paid to
student interns later than 60 days after employment at the Financial Educational
Center has ended.
