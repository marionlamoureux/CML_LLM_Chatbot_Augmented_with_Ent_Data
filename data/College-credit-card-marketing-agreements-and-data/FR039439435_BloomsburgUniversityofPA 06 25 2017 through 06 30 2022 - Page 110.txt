Detail of each line item stated in the charts above is listed below.

Signing Bonus: A one-time unrestricted bonus payment to be presented within the first 90 days
of the first year that this contract is in effect.

Unrestricted Anniversary Bonus: An unrestricted renewal bonus to be presented within the first
90 days of the second, third, fourth, and fifth years this contract is in effect.

Scholarships: Funds to be used for scholarship purposes administered entirely by Bloomsburg
University for academic or sports-related needs, to be awarded each year to Bloomsburg
students who are also PSECU members. Such scholarship funds shall be presented directly to
Bloomsburg University. The number of scholarships and the amounts per scholarships are to be
agreed upon by Bloomsburg University and PSECU.

Miscellaneous Sponsorships: Sponsorships are to be agreed upon by Bloomsburg University and
PSECU.
