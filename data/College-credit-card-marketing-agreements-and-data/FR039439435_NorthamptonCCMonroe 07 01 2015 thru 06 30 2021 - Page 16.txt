Incentive Schedule

e Incentives are payable for any of the services (listed in the chart below)
established at the Financial Educational Center upon opening. Incentives
will be paid for existing members adding the following services at the
Financial Educational Center within one (1) year of account opening.

e Incentives are payable for membership or services requested as a result of
orientation events; special mailings by Northampton Community College,
Monroe Campus (postage paid and materials provided by Credit Union); or if
requested at the Financial Educational Center. Incentives will be paid for
existing members adding the following services at the Financial Educational
Center within one (1) year of account opening.

e Northampton Community College, Monroe Campus incentive payments will
be made no later than the 25" of the following month.

e Financial Educational Center Management Intern and Financial Educational
Center Student Intern incentives are payable to the intern during his term of
employment at the Financial Educational Center. Student Intern incentive
payments will be made as a part of the normal Credit Union payroll process.
No incentive will be paid to student interns later than 60 days after

employment at the Financial Educational Center has ended.
