Addendum to the Harrisburg Area Community College, Harrisburg Campus Financial
Educational Center Agreement dated July 1, 2015
Between Pennsylvania State Employees Credit Union & Harrisburg Area Community
College, Harrisburg Campus

THIS ADDENDUM (the ?Addendum?), made this 8th day of July, 2020 (?Addendum Effective
Date?), between the Pennsylvania State Employees Credit Union (?PSECU? or ?Credit Union?),
and Harrisburg Area Community College, Harrisburg Campus (?HACC?)

It is the desire of PSECU and HACC who are the same parties to the ?Harrisburg Area
Community College, Harrisburg Campus Financial Educational Center Agreement?, along with
any addenda and exhibits, all of which were dated July ist, 2015, and were duly executed by
representatives of the aforementioned parties (referred to herein as the ?Amendment?), to
extend the term of the Agreement. NOW THERFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. The parties agree to extend the term of the Agreement for one year until June 30, 2021.

2. For the term of the Addendum, the parties agree Attachment B-Part 2 of the Agreement
is hereby addended to read as follows:

During the term of the Addendum, the parties agree to the following payments:

e Signing Bonus in the amount of $2,000.00 to be paid on or within thirty (30) days
after July 1, 2020.

e Scholarships to be paid to HACC Foundation in the amount of $7,700.00 to be paid
on or within thirty (30) days after July 1, 2020.

e Sponsorships to be paid to HACC Foundation in the amount of $2,000.00 to be paid
on or within thirty (30) days after July 1, 2020.

e Orientation Support in the amount of $500.00 to be paid on or within thirty (30) days
after July 1, 2020.

e Restricted Contribution for the Harrisburg Campus Student Emergency Assistance
Fund to be paid to HACC Foundation in the amount of $5,000.00 to be paid on or
within thirty (30) days after July 1, 2020.

e Rental Payment for Campus Space in the amount of $400.00 per month to begin on
January 1, 2021. Rental Payments shall be paid no later than thirty (30) days after
the end of each month for the preceding month. Notwithstanding the foregoing,
Rental Payments shall not be due for any month in which the physical campus and
Campus Space are not fully open to students.

The Agreement remains in full force and effect in accordance with its terms, except as herein
identified.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have caused this Addendum to the Agreement to
be executed and sealed by their duly authorized representatives as of the date first written
above.
